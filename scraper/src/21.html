We’re looking for partners

The Host Society will be working in partnership with national, provincial and territorial sport organizations, provincial and territorial governments and the private sector to ensure the Canada Games are a relevant and integral component of the developing athlete’s pursuit of excellence in every sport on the Games program.

Contributing to the promise of providing opportunities to improve the local economy, it is the desire of the 2022 Canada Games to engage and partner with organizations operating within the Niagara Region. However, 2022 Canada Games will intend to negotiate contract terms with the most qualified partner that achieve the goals of the 2022 Niagara Summer Canada Games regardless of geographic location. In partnership with the Niagara Region; all previous and current RFPs are hosted and promoted via their economic division. Should you wish to be added to their list of vetted suppliers, please connect with Valerie Kuhns, Manager, Strategic Initiatives, Niagara Region at Valerie.kuhns@niagararegion.ca

Requests for Proposal are also hosted at GNCC for members of the Greater Niagara Chamber of Commerce.
