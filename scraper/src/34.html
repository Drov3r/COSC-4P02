Niagara 2022 Tickets

Tickets On Sale Now

Be the first to get your tickets to attend the Niagara 2022 Canada Summer Games, which are scheduled from August 6-21, 2022! This is a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to attend just the third Canada Games ever held in Ontario.

Purchase tickets

By purchasing your All-Access Sports Pass or Sport Specific Week Pass, you will have a front row seat to the highest level of national competition for thousands of up-and-coming Canadian athletes. The Games act as a showcase for Canada’s next generation of national, international, Olympic, Paralympic and Special Olympic champions. Alumni of the Canada Summer Games feature the likes of Andre De Grasse, Kylie Masse, Desiree Scott, Steve Nash, Stephanie Dixon, Katarina Roxon and Brent Lakatos just to name a few.

For group orders (minimum 10 tickets) or for more information, contact Brianne Whitty, Manager, Ticket Sales & Merchandise at bwhitty@2022canadagames.ca or (905) 932-7384.

Frequently Asked Questions

What tickets are currently being sold for the 2022 Canada Summer Games?

The All-Access Sports Pass and the Sport-Specific Week Pass went on sale to the public on October 15, 2021. Both passes remain available for purchase online.

The All-Access Sport Pass provides a ticket holder with access to all sports played across both weeks with the exception of swimming and diving (due to limited capacity).

The Sport-Specific Week Pass grants a ticket holder with access to all competitions for one sport during one week of the 2022 Canada Games.

I want to purchase tickets for specific days of the Games. What kind of passes will be available for that and when will they go on sale?

All-Access Day Passes, Sport-Specific Day Passes and Opening Ceremony tickets will go on sale in May 2022.

All-Access Day Passes will grant ticket holders access to all sport competitions occurring during a single day, with the exception of swimming and diving (due to limited capacity). This type of pass will only be made available during the first four days of competition for a given week of the 2022 Canada Games.

Separately, Sport-Specific Day Passes will grant a ticket holder access to all competitions for one sport during a single day of competition. This type of day pass will be available on all days of competition with the exception of a medal day in team sports. If interested in attending a single day of medal competition for a team sport, we recommend that you buy a Sport-Specific Medal Day Pass instead, which also will be made available for purchase at the same time as these other passes.

Finally, the Opening Ceremony, which will mark the beginning of the Niagara 2022 Canada Summer Games, will be a ticketed-event that will take place on Saturday, August 6, 2022 at the Meridian Centre in St. Catharines.

Are medal rounds or sessions included in the All-Access Sports Pass and the Sport-Specific Week passes?

Yes. All medal rounds/sessions are included as part of these passes.

What is the COVID-19 policy for spectators?

Currently, no official decision has been made on a COVID-19 policy for spectators who will attend the Niagara 2022 Canada Summer Games. The Games’ events and its spectators will be subject to public health requirements from the federal, provincial and Niagara Region governments, along with the policies of Niagara 2022’s competition and non-competition venues at the time of the event between August 6-21, 2022.

We will closely monitor all dialogue and decisions made by the different levels of government as it pertains to future events and programming. We will issue pertinent updates regarding spectator policies as we receive them.

Are discounts available for students, seniors, or children?

Children 5 years of age and under are admitted free of charge and do not require a ticket for entry. Only one child is permitted per paying adult. Depending on venue capacity, the child may be required to sit on the adult’s lap. Seniors, students and children older than five years of age will be required to purchase tickets at full price.

When do individual sporting event tickets go on sale?

Tickets for individual sporting events will go on sale when the Sport-Specific Day Passes are made available for purchase in May 2022.

If I want to go watch a single baseball game or a soccer match, how do I get tickets for just one game?

Sport-Specific Day Passes will be available for individual ticketed sports through our website starting May 2022. A Sport-Specific Day Pass will allow you to watch all of the events scheduled on one particular day, for one particular sport. In order to watch one specific game, the Sport-Specific Day Pass will be what you want to buy (it just means you will also get to watch the rest of that day’s games/competitions for that sport).

Do I have to buy tickets online ahead of time or can I buy them in-person at a box office?

More information about in-person options for buying tickets will be shared in May 2022. There will also be box office locations at each of our ticketed sports venues during the 2022 Canada Games for in-person ticket sales (based on availability).

Will my tickets provide me with an assigned seat or is it general admission?

All Niagara 2022 sporting events are general admission. Only the Opening Ceremony will have assigned seating.

When will I receive my tickets for the pass(es) or packages I purchased?

All-Access Sports Passes and Sport-Specific Week Passes will be mailed out starting May 2022 and continue through mid-July 2022. Any passes purchased from mid-July through the start of the Games will need to be picked up in-person at our main box office location (details to follow regarding location and hours of operation closer to Games time).

What if I move before my tickets are mailed in May 2022?

If your email or mailing address changes before tickets are sent out, please update your profile information in your Tournkey account to ensure delivery to the correct address. Alternatively, you may contact Brianne Whitty, Manager of Ticketing & Merchandise at bwhitty@2022canadagames.ca.

What if my tickets are lost, misplaced or stolen?

In the event of a lost, misplaced or stolen ticket prior to the Games, please contact Brianne Whitty at bwhitty@2022canadagames.ca. If they are lost, misplaced or stolen during the Games, please visit the main box office (location and hours or operation to be confirmed closer to Games time). Please note that a replacement fee of $25.00 will be assessed.

I have accessible / mobility needs, how can I have them addressed?

When purchasing your pass or package through the 2022 Canada Summer Games platform, click on the “Accessible Seating” button and continue your purchase. When seats are assigned, you will be placed in an accessible location and notified of your exact seating location via email.

Service dogs are allowed at all venues, unless otherwise prohibited by law. Service animals need to be clearly identified and have up-to-date documentation. If you have any questions regarding seating arrangements or pet relief areas, contact Brianne Whitty at bwhitty@2022canadagames.ca, as the answer will depend on the venue.

Where can I purchase tickets for the Niagara 2022 Canada Summer Games?

You can buy your tickets to Niagara 2022 by visiting niagara2022games.ca/tickets and clicking on the ‘Purchase Tickets’ button, which will take you to our online platform on Tournkey, the Official Ticketing Partner of the 2022 Canada Games.

Currently all tickets must be purchased through our online Tournkey platform. More information will follow in May 2022 in regards to in-person ticket buying options.

For more information about the Games themselves and the various events that will take place at the Niagara 2022 Canada Summer Games, we recommend that you visit our website at niagara2022games.ca/

Are there any free or non-ticketed events taking place?

The following sports will be non-ticketed: cycling, golf and sailing. Please be advised that there will be no formal seating areas at these events, rather standing room areas will be designated for those interested in attending these three sports.

Additionally, Niagara 2022 will feature two premier festival events, Niagara Place and the 13 For 13 Cultural Festival, which will both offer complimentary admission to everyone. For more information about both, we’d recommend that you please visit niagara2022games.ca/events/niagara-place/ and niagara2022games.ca/events/13for13/

Meanwhile, the Closing Ceremony that will cap off the 2022 Canada Games on Sunday, August 21, 2022 at Queen Victoria Park in Niagara Falls will also be fully complimentary and open to the public.

Are there All-Access Single Week Passes?

No. We suggest purchasing a Sport-Specific Week Pass to the sport you most want to watch. Once day passes go on sale in May 2022, you may purchase an All-Access Day Pass or a Sport-Specific Day Pass for the other sports that interest you.

Are All-Access Passes Transferable?

No, they are not transferable. All-Access Passes can only be used by the person whose name and picture are on the pass itself. Photo identification on each All-Access Sport Pass will be checked at the point of entry to each venue.
