Applications Are Open!

Apply to become one of 4,500 Niagara 2022 Canada Summer Games volunteers.

Before We Begin...

We look forward to your application to volunteer at the Niagara 2022 Canada Summer Games. There are a few things we want you to know before you proceed. Please read these points carefully. 

  • All volunteers must be fully vaccinated in order to apply. You must have completed a series of COVID-19 vaccinations that have been approved by Health Canada, or have obtained a valid Medical or Religious/Creed exemption (documented proof will be requested at a later date).
  • You must be 16 years or older as of July 1, 2022 to volunteer.
  • You will be required to volunteer a minimum of 36 hours during the Games
  • You will have the opportunity to select your top three preferences and although we will try very hard to place you into a preferred volunteer role and location, there are no guarantees as we may have additional operational needs. 
  • We will be asking for your size of uniform in the application form. Please refer to the Uniform sizing chart Women's and Uniform sizing chart Men's.
  • You can visit us at the Meridian Volunteer Centre located at the Pen Centre  Pen Centre 221 Glendale Ave. St. Catharines, ON  L2T 2K9 if you require assistance completing your application form or email us at volunteer@2022canadagames.ca
  • For more information please read our Frequently Asked Questions.
  • Please note that all volunteers must apply through this application site even if you previously signed up for our newsletter.
  • For best functionality please use a laptop or PC to complete the application. It is recommended that you do not use a mobile device.

About the Roles

There are many types of roles in a large variety of areas. Please refer to this document to look through the functional areas and understand the tasks assigned each.

It will take approximately 20 minutes to complete this application. Once your user profile has been created (ID number generated), you can leave and come back to complete the application process at another time by logging in with your N22 Email and Password.

Volunteer: Frequently Asked Questions

What are the Niagara 2022 Canada Summer Games?

The Canada Games, held every two years, alternating between summer and winter, are the largest multi-sport event in the country and represent the pinnacle of interprovincial/territorial sport competition. The Niagara 2022 Canada Summer Games encompasses 13 municipalities (12 plus 1 region) and will be held 2022. The Games will feature 18 sports, 5,000 participants, over 4,000 volunteers and various cultural events. In 2022, there will be opportunities for para-athletes and Special Olympic athletes to compete as well.

What sports will be part of the Niagara 2022 Canada Summer Games and where will they be held?

A complete list of sports that will be part of Niagara 2022 Canada Summer Games is available here. Sports will be held in 18 venues across the Niagara region. The complete list of venues is available here.

What types of volunteer roles are available with the Games and how do I apply to volunteer?

There are three different types of volunteer roles available with the Games:

  1. Planning Volunteers: These are volunteers who will help us in the planning stages leading up to the Games. You can view a list of current Planning Volunteer vacancies and apply via our website.

  2. Volunteers at Pre-Games Events: Various events will be held from now until Games-time and many volunteers will be needed. We will post this opportunity as needed on our website for people to express interest in helping and they will join a “pool” of volunteers that we can call upon to help at these pre-Games events.

  3. Games-time Volunteers: These are volunteers who will assist during the actual Games in August 2022. Our online volunteer application site will open October 25, 2021, and all volunteers will be required to apply, and will go through a screening and selection process before being assigned to a role, scheduled, oriented, and provided with a uniform.

What is the difference between pre-Games and Games-time?

Pre-Games: This is from now until the actual Games.

Games-time: Refers to when the Games will be held, including the Opening and Closing Ceremonies.

What is the volunteer recruitment timeline?

Planning Volunteers: Positions will be posted on the website, as needed. Volunteers for Pre-Games Events: Positions will be posted on the website, as needed Games-time Volunteers. The Volunteer application website is now taking applications.

What is the minimum time commitment for volunteering at the Games?

All volunteers that sign up for Games-time are expected to volunteer at least 36 hours from August 6, 2022 - August 21, 2022. Some may do that all in one week, and others may spread it across the two weeks of the Games.

What types of volunteer positions are available for Games-time?

There are more than 150 unique volunteer roles available during Games-time that will support many areas from Sport Operations to Transportation, from Ticketing to Food Service, from Event Services to Merchandise Sales, just to name a few. Our Games-time volunteer application website is now taking applications. All volunteers will be required to register online and move through our screening, selection, assignment, and training process.

When I apply to volunteer, will I be able to choose what venue I am stationed at?

When applying to be a volunteer, everyone will be given the opportunity to choose their preferences for venue locations and their top 3 choices for volunteer roles. The N22 team will do their best to provide volunteers with a preferred role at their preferred venue.

How do I sign up to volunteer for different events leading up to the Games?

All pre-Games volunteer vacancies will be posted on our website here as they become available.

I have a disability. Can I volunteer with the Games? Can I volunteer with my aid?

Yes and yes! We welcome people of all abilities to apply for a volunteer position with the Games and we will work with you to determine how we can support you in your role. Please note, if you are volunteering with your aid/caregiver, they too will have to register on our Games-time volunteer registration portal.

Can I bring my service dog with me?

Yes! We welcome people of all abilities to apply for a volunteer role with the Games and we will work with you to determine how we can support you in your role.

I live outside of the Niagara region, can I still volunteer?

Yes! Please note: All Games-time volunteers will be required to register online and are responsible for their own transportation and accommodation arrangements.

I live outside of Canada. Can I still volunteer during the Games?

Yes! Please note: All volunteers will be required to register online and are responsible for their own transportation and accommodation arrangements. The Niagara 2022 Canada Summer Games Host Society is not responsible for arranging any travel documentation including visas.

What is the language requirement to be a volunteer with the Games?

The Games will be hosted in both official languages. Therefore, we will be recruiting English-speaking and bilingual (English and French) volunteers. Applicants who indicate French language capability on their application will be required to demonstrate proficiency through a language proficiency screening process.

I do not drive. Can I still volunteer?

Yes! Please note: volunteers are responsible for their own transportation arrangements.

Do I need to have special qualifications or experience to volunteer for the Games?

Certain volunteer positions require certifications or licenses. Depending upon the role, volunteers may be required to submit appropriate documentation. Volunteer position descriptions will include any qualification requirements. An example of a role that will require a certification is a volunteer driver who will require a valid Ontario driver’s license and recent driver’s abstract, or a merchandise volunteer who handles money will need to complete a police and credit check.

Do you have group volunteering opportunities available with the Games?

Yes, we do. Please get in touch with us at: volunteer@2022canadagames.ca to discuss group volunteering opportunities with the Games.

How do I learn more about the Niagara 2022 Canada Summer Games?

Sign up for our monthly newsletter here. Be sure to use an email address that you regularly use and check. Also, mark it “safe” to receive email communications from Niagara 2022 in your inbox.

When does the Games-time volunteer application open?

We encourage people who are interested in volunteering to visit our website for information. Our online application website is now taking applications.

Can my children volunteer with the Games?

Yes, Niagara 2022 Canada Summer Games volunteers must be at least 16 years of age by July 1st, 2022. This decision is based on historical data from previous Canada Games. Those between the ages of 16 and 18 years are required to complete a parent/guardian consent form during their screening process.

How long are volunteer shifts during the day?

An average volunteer shift is between 4 - 8 hours. Shifts will depend upon sport and venue schedules and will vary for each volunteer position. For example, an usher may need to start their shift 30 minutes before the venue opens to the public, while a medical volunteer may be required to start their shift 30 minutes before the competition begins.

Where is the volunteer centre located?

The Meridian Volunteer Centre is located at the Pen Centre in St. Catharines at entrance 6. The Meridian Volunteer Centre is open with posted hours of operation that will gradually increase as we get closer to the Games. Visitors to the Centre can have their questions answered and can receive assistance with their volunteer application, and in Spring 2022, the space will be converted into the uniform and accreditation distribution centre. (221 Glendale Ave; Unit 617L, St. Catharines, ON, L2T 2K9)

What safety/COVID-19 protocols are in place for people who wish to visit the Meridian Volunteer Centre?

Visitors to the Meridian Volunteer Centre will be required to wear a mask or face covering during their visit, sanitize their hands and complete a self-declaration form upon entry.

Are vaccines mandatory for volunteers?

Yes, in response to the provincial government and local public health directives, we have implemented a mandatory vaccine policy for all of our staff and volunteers. Proof of full vaccination must be provided prior to working or volunteering at a N22 location, including the offices, venues, warehouse, etc. Details will be shared through the volunteer application site. Exemptions will be considered for medical or religious/creed requirements with appropriate documentation.

Will volunteers receive/need to wear a uniform?

Yes, all Games-time volunteers will receive a high-quality uniform that will be handed out, along with an accreditation badge, closer to Games-time.

Are volunteers required to find their own transportation to/from their shifts?

Yes, volunteers are responsible for their own transportation to and from their shifts.

Can high school aged volunteers receive volunteer hours as part of their requirement to graduate?

Yes, the Niagara 2022 team will support this process for high school students. Niagara 2022 Canada Summer Games volunteers must be at least 16 years of age by July 1st, 2022. Those between the ages of 16 and 18 years are required to complete a parent/guardian consent form during their screening process.

When can I buy tickets for the Niagara 2022 Canada Summer Games?

Tickets go on sale October 15, 2021 through the N22 website. Day Passes and Opening Ceremony tickets will go on sale in the Spring. There are currently two types of passes available for sale:

  • All-Access Sports Pass $200 + ticket fees
  • Sport Specific Week Pass
